Coffee Arabica

This variety emerged in the mountains of Ethiopia, Africa, and it is no coincidence that one of the main recommendations for planting this coffee is to consider the altitude of the place. The higher it is grown, the better the grain quality. Usually, drinks considered special are made with Arabica coffee which, despite having a lower caffeine content, has a very particular complexity of aromas.
